Please find a digital copy of the "Reply to Reviews Guideline" 
saved onto this diskette in Rich Text Format.  This file should be 
readable by most word-processing programs being run by most operating 
systems.  We ask that you add your responses to the referees' 
comments in the column spaces provided and save your additions in
Rich Text Format onto this same diskette.  Please return this diskette 
to our journal office when you return your revised manuscript.  If you were also 
instructed to send us an electronic version of the revised manuscript,
the manuscript should be saved onto a separate diskette.
